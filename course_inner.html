Exploring Errors With God's Love

Rs.1200

Introduction

This course offers a special outlook at our life’s shortcomings through the lens of a God-loving seeker. Based on the verses of Shrimadji’s “He Prabhu!” Kaavya written in 1890s, this introspective study is for anyone who wants to take a broader look at life and improve the strength of inner love.

Key Features

Step-by-step understanding of our inner shortcomings with easy antidotes.

A deeper insight into Shrimadji's intense yet loving call to God through His 20-verse poem.

Creative charts, introspective questions and quizzes.

Indulge into higher questions towards life through more than 20 videos split into 5 modules.
