About

SwarajKriya

“Meditation is my most important offering.”

sri guru

SwaRaj Kriya, a yogic meditation practice designed by Sri Guru, operates at deeper levels of human existence to inspire a spiritual awakening amongst the masses. Since its inception in 2015, the Kriya has been at the center of the mission as the fuel that powers the individual and collective evolution of its seekers. It is a dynamic process that has evolved over the years to ensure each practitioner experiences growth across multiple facets of life.

The Swaraj Kriya Program offers an app-based guided meditation track and some key resources that people can use to practice regularly. Each practitioner is assigned a personalized program that best compliments their daily spiritual activities. Further, people are organized in groups with appointed leaders who guide them, inspire them and keep a firm follow-up through periodic one-on-one meetings. As of April 2023, SwaRaj Kriya has been performed for over 5,22,495 times by the program members.

“It is not just a Meditation. The Kriya is a tool to evolve and stay in tune to a much higher consciousness. It is an art that needs practice, and a science that needs understanding.”

sri guru

The Science

While Sri Guru’s all-encompassing talks impart an intellectual clarity of the path, it is the practice of meditation techniques, specifically SwaRaj Kriya, that enables people to understand the intent of her talks on an experiential level.

The Kriya is a yogic technique that utilizes:

Breath Zone

Sound Zone

Energy Zone

This is through a carefully constructed process to expand the level of individual consciousness. Consistent practitioners of SwaRaj Kriya have listed its many benefits.

Physical Benefits

Higher energy levels all day long.

Calm and rhythmic breath pattern.

Spiritual Benefits

Inherent feeling of satisfaction.

Virtues of forgiveness, acceptance, gratitude and love towards all.

An intention to return to the society.

Experience of boundless joy.

Mental & Emotional Benefits

Reduced stress and anxiety.

Improved emotional quotient.

Clarity of thought and action through strengthened introspection.

Sound and healthy relationships with Self and others.
