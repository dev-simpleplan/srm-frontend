Biography

Sri Guru Ratna Prabhu, founder of Shrimad Rajchandra Mission Delhi, is a master of the spiritual process, guiding thousands of seekers on their journey to self-realization. Born in 1973 to a Jain family, Sri Guru defies sectarianism. Through more than thousands of talks, she has explored all 4 major religions of eastern culture — Hinduism, Jainism, Buddhism and Sikhism. Her distinctive voice inspires people to go beyond mundane religious practices and explore their core purpose — realizing our true identity.

“Preparing religions for the next generation.”

sri guru

With over 1200 live talks worldwide since 2010, Sri Guru has underscored the need for religions to evolve with logic and clarity to meet the needs of the current and future generations. Demonstrating her commitment to this message, Sri Guru has expounded the core meanings of more than 20 scriptures including Ashtavakra Gita, Bhagavad Gita, Uttaradhyayan Sutra, Dhammapada, Bhaktamar Stotra, Japji Sahib, Yogvashishth Maharamayana, and many more. Through these talks, she breaks through superficial interpretations, bringing forth the universal spiritual essence of these sacred texts.

We need clarity in life, not certainty

Sri Guru's talks are designed to provide seekers with absolute clarity through the inclusion of scientific explanations, comprehensive visuals, and live demonstrations. This approach ensures that audiences of all generations are able grasp the subject matter easily, making the content actionable in their daily lives.

Sri Guru: The Meditation Artist.

Along with her intellectual brilliance, Sri Guru is widely revered to be an artist of meditation. The numerous meditation practices devised by her enable the modern-day seeker to release the perceived stress of the world, elevate their mental and spiritual well-being, and allow unconditional happiness to blossom within. Sri Guru says,

“I cannot talk you into awakening. You have to go through a process.”

sri guru

And she is always absolutely clear about the process.

“One second of actual plunge into Meditation puts together the effect of years of knowledge-collection. For within that one experience belongs the reason for all your knowledge”

sri guru
